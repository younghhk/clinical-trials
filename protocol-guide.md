# Clinical Trial Protocol Starter Guide

A practical checklist for researchers preparing a clinical trial protocol.

## 1. Clarify the Research Question
- Define the main objective.
- Identify whether the trial is therapeutic or non therapeutic.
- Specify the phase (I, II, or III).
- Provide a brief rationale.

## 2. Define Key Trial Elements
- Population criteria.
- Intervention details.
- Comparator.
- Outcomes.
- Estimand.

## 3. Choose the Study Design
- Randomized or single arm.
- Blinded or open label.
- Parallel, crossover, or adaptive.
- Use of stratification.

## 4. Safety and Monitoring Plan
- Dose limiting toxicities.
- Maximum tolerated dose evaluation.
- Interim monitoring.
- Data Monitoring Committee.

## 5. Statistical Considerations
- Sample size and power.
- Effect size assumptions.
- Handling missing data.
- Interim analysis methods.
- Statistical analysis plan outline.

## 6. Randomization
- Method and ratio.
- Allocation concealment.

## 7. Operational Details
- Visit schedule.
- Data collection plans.
- Protocol deviation handling.
- Accrual monitoring.

## 8. Ethics and Regulatory
- Informed consent.
- IRB submission.
- Safety reporting.
- Regulatory documentation.

## 9. Protocol Structure
A full protocol typically includes:
- Title and synopsis  
- Background  
- Objectives  
- Methods  
- Eligibility  
- Intervention  
- Outcomes  
- SAP  
- Safety  
- Administration
